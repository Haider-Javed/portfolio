About Me

My name is Haider Javed, and I was born in a backward village near Sialkot where I completed my early education with very limited resources. Despite these challenges, my passion for learning and my strong desire to excel academically kept me motivated from an early age. My keen enthusiasm for modern world technology pushed me to explore computers, problem solving, and the rapidly evolving field of technology on my own. This self-driven motivation helped me secure All Pakistan Rank 18 in the NUST Entrance Test, marking a major milestone in my academic journey. I joined NUST and began my journey of learning, experimenting, and exploring the world of technology with a disciplined and growth-oriented mindset. I have strong problem-solving skills and solved more than 300+ LeetCode problems in my very first semester, strengthening my foundations in data structures and algorithms. Alongside academics, I am a self-taught web developer and have built several meaningful projects by applying modern development practices. I am also a game developer and have created multiple games, which helped me develop logical thinking, creativity, and a deeper understanding of system design. I continuously push the boundaries of my brain to understand modern technologies, improve my skills, and stay aligned with industry standards. Looking forward, my goal is to use computer science to create impact at scale and contribute to innovative solutions that solve real-world problems. Coming from humble beginnings has kept me grounded, disciplined, and focused as I move forward with a clear vision and long-term commitment to excellence.
